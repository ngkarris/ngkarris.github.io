Hello!

I am a fourth year Mathematics PhD student at UC San Diego. My advisor is Alex Cloninger.

Very broadly, my interests center around data science and machine learning. Most of my current work involves applying (linearized) optimal transport to problems relating to particle system simulations, hyperspectral imaging, and diffusion models. See Research for more detail and a list of my papers.

I graduated from Northwestern University in 2021 with a BA in Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science. I wrote an honors thesis advised by Ezra Getzler.
